👋 Hola, Good Day!

I am a software engineer with 7 years of experience designing and scaling distributed systems, real-time data platforms, and fintech infrastructure. I specialize in event-driven architectures, usage-based billing systems, identity and access management, and subscription lifecycle orchestration.

Much of my work centers on building fault-tolerant systems that hold up under load — idempotency guarantees, backpressure handling, retry-safe processing, and eventual consistency. At Fynd, I architected a distributed real-time metering system processing thousands of events per second and co-built a centralized control plane unifying authentication, authorization, subscriptions, and payment orchestration across products.

My technical foundation is primarily Python and JavaScript, working across Django, Flask, Node.js, and Express on the backend and React and Vue on the frontend. I'm comfortable across the stack — from Kafka pipelines and DAG-based orchestration to PostgreSQL and multi-tenant data modeling.

Before fintech, I worked on bank and KYC integrations at Decentro, document-processing pipelines at Linarc, and natural language understanding systems early in my career. That ML and NLP background still shapes how I think about data and systems.

I care about clean, maintainable code, clear contracts between services, and systems that fail gracefully. I was a Google Summer of Code student with Joomla and have been recognized with Fynd's Star Award for resolving critical system bottlenecks.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me — I'm always happy to connect with fellow engineers and talk distributed systems, billing platforms, or anything in between.
